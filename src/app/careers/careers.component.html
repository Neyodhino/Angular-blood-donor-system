Introducing our values

We are a team driven by our values. Everyday we strive to fulfill these values in everything we do. All LifeBankers must commit to these set of values and this is the secret sauce of what makes working at LifeBank awesome.

You must care for our patients, our people and our partners.

At LifeBank, you must care for our patients, our people (team members) and our partners. Caring for patients, means focusing on impact and doing all we can to rescue people on their worst day. We are the watchers on the wall, and the shield that guards. We care about each other (people), we don’t do office politics, and we don’t work with assholes. We care about our partners, because we believe it is best when everyone wins together.

We are relentless in the pursuit of our goals. We are the beehive!

At LifeBank, it is our duty to rescue the most vulnerable members of our community. We must rescue pregnant women who are bleeding, little kids who need oxygen, newborns who need vaccines, and many more critical ill people and we must do this at scale. We are a team that must deliver operational excellence through sheer consistent effort. This requires a relentless focus and effort from every LifeBanker. We are the beehive.

We believe deeply in the growth mindset - Our cleaner must be preparing for the CEO role.

At LifeBank, we learn. We are never satisfied with the status quo. Our work is never finished. Everyday, we are committed to our personal growth and by extension the growth of the business. We believe anything can happen, as long as you are committed to learning and to doing the work. That is what truly matters to us. You must be committed to your personal growth, and be driven by a learning spirit.

We believe in merit. Only the excellent survive.

At LifeBank, office politics bore us. We don't play favorites and your growth in the business is not at all dependent on who likes you and who doesn't. Your growth is entirely dependent on you. If you live within our values, and deliver value to LifeBank consistently, we owe you growth that comes with financial rewards.

Chief Finance Officer

The CFO will be responsible for overseeing all the financial operations of LifeBank, including accounting, financial reporting, tax, business control and treasury. S/he will direct the company’s financial objectives, control the company’s finances, establish financial policies and manage financial risk.
